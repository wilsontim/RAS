Today's Refrigerant Life Cycle

Refrigerant is treated as a consumable, on a linear path… produce, use and eventually emit.

Refrigerant Life Cycle with RAS

Refrigerant and data are tracked across the supply chain, enabling your refrigerant to become your asset.

The Refrigerant Asset System from EOS Climate provides all participants in the refrigerant value chain with an unprecedented level of transparency around the location, volume, usage, and value of all refrigerants. Using proprietary mobile technology and integrating data in real time, EOS Climate’s Refrigerant Asset System puts you in control of your refrigerant assets, turning question marks into clarity, confusion into control, and enabling you to generate economic value throughout the lifecycle. From the initial purchase of virgin refrigerants all the way to the banking of recycled assets, you have the power to be EPA compliant at the touch of a button, to identify potential leak issues through exception reporting, to assess your inventory on demand, to plan for future growth and be prepared for an evolving regulatory climate. Powerful data analytics help you ensure that if anyone profits from the asset, it’s you.
